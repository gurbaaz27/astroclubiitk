Takneek

Initiated a few years back,Takneek is IIT Kanpur Inter-Hall SnT competition event.

As much as it is an arena of tech battles, it serves as a platform to introduce the freshers batch to various SnT clubs.

The seniors educate them from scratch, and it is the enthusiasm which counts.

Astronomy Club conducts variety of fun events: from space riddles to identification in night sky,from physics and mathematical numericals to hypothetical situations, the themes are ever-engaging.
